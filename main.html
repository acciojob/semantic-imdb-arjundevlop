Summary of the Movie
The story of an Indian Muslim man who suffers from the Asperger's syndrome, a form of autism that impacts social interaction abilities. He is arrested mistakenly as a suspected terrorist in post-9/11 Los Angeles.

Actor -

Shah Rukh Khan

Shah rukh Khan played the role of Rizwan Khan, a Muslim from the Borivali section of Mumbai, suffers from Asperger's syndrome, a form of high-functioning autism that complicates socialization.
